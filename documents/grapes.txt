Grapes are small, round fruits that grow in clusters on vines and are widely cultivated around the world. They come in various colors, including green, red, black, yellow, and purple, and are known for their sweet or tart flavor. Grapes are rich in vitamins, antioxidants, and other beneficial nutrients, making them a healthy snack choice. They can be eaten fresh, dried into raisins, or processed into products like grape juice, jelly, and wine. Due to their versatility and nutritional value, grapes have been enjoyed by humans for thousands of years and play a significant role in both culinary and agricultural industries.
